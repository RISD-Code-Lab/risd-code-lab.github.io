Weekly Session Signup

RISD Code Lab provides a space for inquiry into creative programming. It is open to all students.

This year, we are dividing up Code Lab into 2 types: artist workshops and regular weekly sessions. Artist workshops feature a visiting designer, who will be hosting a special session related to their practice. Other weekly sessions serve as extra office hours, where an instructor will be available to provide technical support for your projects.

You can check out tutorials from previous sessions of Code Lab in 2021 and 2020, which were organized around certain project themes every week.
